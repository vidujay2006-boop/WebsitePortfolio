Biography

Early Life

Kurt Donald Cobain was born on 20 February 1967 in Washington, USA. Throughout his childhood, Cobain developed a strong interest with music, singing and playing instruments through cultural and envionmental factors encouraged by family. Growing up listening to The Beatles, Rock n' Roll, Punk Rock, these factors paved the way for his career path.

Formation of Nirvana

Nirvana's journey began years earlier in Aberdeen high school, where Kurt met fellow band-mate and student Krist Novoselic - who both found a mutual interest in rock and underground music. Novoselic was hesitant at first to start a band with Cobain, especially due to the rise of Rock n' Roll at the time, however after listening to one of the latter's demo tapes, he agreed. Throughout the late 80s, Kurt and Krist would practise and record ideas in their home studio.
